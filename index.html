What it does

Everything a pregnant woman
needs, in one place

Designed with nurses and mothers in mind — MaternAlert is not just an app, it's a companion through every week of your pregnancy.

🩺
Blood Pressure Tracker

Log readings daily. Detects patterns and alerts you the moment BP reaches concerning levels.

🚨
Emergency Alert Screen

Full-screen emergency mode with one-tap calling to your saved emergency contacts when danger is detected.

📍
Nearby Clinic Finder

Uses your location to surface hospitals and clinics near you instantly — critical in late-night emergencies.

📅
Auto Pregnancy Calendar

Your pregnancy week updates automatically every day from your due date. No manual tracking needed.

👤
Smart Risk Profile

Captures hereditary history, previous pregnancies, and gestation to personalise your risk level from day one.

Education Hub

Plain-language articles on pre-eclampsia signs, causes, and what to do — accessible anytime.
